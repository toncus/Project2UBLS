United States Labor Statistics

This website is designed to provide interactive visualizations of current labor breakdowns in the United States and create predictions based on current trands. Using public data from the U.S. Department of Labor Statistics, we created an interactive dashboard to show the labor breakdown of each state along with available historical data.

Select a state by clicking on it below to see a comparison of what industries are most people working in that state. To view a breakdown of the labor categories, select the 'Classification Breakdown' above.
